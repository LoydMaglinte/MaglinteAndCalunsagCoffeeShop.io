Macchiato

A shot of espresso "stained" or "marked" with a small amount of frothy milk, creating a bold and concentrated flavor profile.

Mocha

PHP 160

A decadent coffee beverage combining espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate, often topped with whipped cream, offering a delightful blend of rich flavors.

Coffee Latte

A smooth and creamy coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and a larger amount of steamed milk, topped with a small layer of frothy milk.

Piccolo Latte

Similar to a latte but with a higher proportion of coffee to milk, creating a smaller, stronger, and more concentrated beverage.

Ristretto

An even more concentrated and shorter shot of espresso, using less water during the brewing process to intensify the coffee flavor.

Affogato

A delightful dessert-like coffee where a shot of hot espresso is poured over a scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato, creating a delicious combination of hot and cold elements.
